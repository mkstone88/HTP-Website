## Why we love Mesta Park

**Mesta Park**, nestled in the heart of our city, is not just another neighborhood; it’s a vibrant testament to historical charm meeting modern living. At Hometown Painting, we have had the privilege of bringing colors to several of its historic homes, witnessing firsthand the architectural interest and community spirit that makes Mesta Park truly unique.

At Hometown Painting, we’re committed to preserving the beauty and integrity of Mesta Park. Whether it’s a fresh coat of paint or a detailed restoration, we approach every project with the respect and care these historic homes deserve.

## About Hometown Painting LLC

#### Our Story – Professional Painters in Oklahoma City

When I started my career in painting, my grandfather gave me his old paint sprayer. It wasn’t much to look at, but it was a hard working machine that helped me paint a lot of houses. To me, it was the perfect symbol of my grandfather passing the torch to me.

I admire the craftsmanship and integrity of my grandfather’s generation of painters. I want to continue this tradition with Hometown Painting.

I feel like this kindred spirit with the past generations is why Hometown Painting has been such a good fit for a neighborhood like Mesta Park which has so much history.

I have noticed that Mesta Park is a very family oriented neighborhood. We’re proud to be a local, family owned and operated company. My wife, Rachel, has played a vital role in making Hometown Painting what it is today.

Please take some more time to learn [about us here](/professional-painters-okc).

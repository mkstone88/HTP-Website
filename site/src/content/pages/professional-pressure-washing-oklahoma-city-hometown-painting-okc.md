After pressure washing, we may apply protective sealants or treatments to extend the cleanliness and protect surfaces from future contamination. For decks and wood surfaces, we can recommend staining or sealing options that complement our painting services.

Our team assesses each surface and uses the correct approach to ensure safety and effectiveness.

The frequency depends on your property’s exposure to elements and contamination. Most Oklahoma City properties benefit from annual pressure washing, though high-traffic areas or properties with significant mold/algae growth may need more frequent service. Professional pressure washing removes most stains effectively, including dirt, algae, mold, and weathering. Some stubborn stains may require pre-treatment with specialized cleaning solutions. Our technicians assess stains and recommend the best approach for your specific situation.

## Starting a Professional Painting Tradition

![Matt's grandfather started his career as a professional painter](/uploads/2022/08/50s-Young-Painter.webp)

Matt’s grandfather, Art, worked as a professional painter most of his life. As a young man, he apprenticed as a painter to support his family. He worked mostly in commercial painting.

Later in life, he decided to start his own company. As you can imagine, Grandpa wondered if he could make it on his own, and with three children to help raise it was no easy decision.

Through hard work and determination, Grandpa built a reputation for professionalism and quality. He painted many homes and businesses over the years and will still tell you stories about some of the difficult jobs he conquered.

We strive to continue the legacy of honest hard work modeled by Art.

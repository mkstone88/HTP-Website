Exterior Staining

Don’t let Oklahoma’s harsh weather ruin your fence, deck or pergola!

Our staining services in Oklahoma City keeps your wood beautiful and protects it against rot, UV damage and moisture.

With a wide range of colors and professional application, you’ll enjoy your outdoor space more and avoid expensive replacements

Benefits of our Exterior Staining Service:

  • Beauty: A gorgeous stained fence creates an inviting backdrop for barbecues and relaxing evenings.
  • Save money: Staining extends the life of your fence, saving thousands in replacement costs.
  • Improve property value: Outdoor living spaces are important to buyers; a well‑maintained fence adds value.
  • Customer‑first service: We provide a free on‑the‑spot quote and keep you informed throughout the job.
  • Expertise: We tailor the process based on your fence’s condition and recommend the best stain from various options.
  • Efficient, careful process: Our training with Wood Defender ensures consistent results, property protection and efficient work.
  • Additional staining services: We stain decks, pergolas and siding too

FAQs – Exterior Painting

How long will stain last? Wood Defender recommends re‑staining every 2–4 years. This is in line with what we have noticed in Oklahoma backyards. Note: semi‑transparent stains last longer than transparent ones.

How long does the process take? Most fences can be stained in one day; if washing is needed, we allow 24–48 hours for the wood to dry.

What should I do before stain day? Move décor or landscaping items away from the fence and turn off sprinklers 24 hours before and after staining.

How will I know when to re‑stain? You may notice fading or a little gray forming on the fence.
